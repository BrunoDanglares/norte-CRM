### PARTE 4 — Adicionar bloco de recorrência no modal

Logo APÓS o bloco de "Data e hora do envio" (antes do `formError` e dos botões), adicione o seguinte JSX:
```tsx
{/* Recorrência */}
<div style={{ marginBottom: 20 }}>
  <label style={{
    display: 'flex', alignItems: 'center', justifyContent: 'space-between',
    cursor: 'pointer', userSelect: 'none',
  }}>
    <div>
      <div style={{ fontSize: 14, fontWeight: 500, color: 'var(--color-text-primary)' }}>
        Disparo recorrente
      </div>
      <div style={{ fontSize: 12, color: 'var(--color-text-secondary)', marginTop: 2 }}>
        Repetir automaticamente todo mês no mesmo dia
      </div>
    </div>
    {/* Toggle switch */}
    <div
      onClick={() => setIsRecurring(v => !v)}
      style={{
        width: 44, height: 24, borderRadius: 12, cursor: 'pointer',
        background: isRecurring ? '#2563eb' : 'var(--color-border-secondary)',
        transition: 'background 0.2s', position: 'relative', flexShrink: 0,
      }}
    >
      <div style={{
        position: 'absolute', top: 3, left: isRecurring ? 23 : 3,
        width: 18, height: 18, borderRadius: '50%', background: '#fff',
        transition: 'left 0.2s',
      }} />
    </div>
  </label>

  {isRecurring && (
    <div style={{
      marginTop: 12, padding: '12px 14px', borderRadius: 8,

      <span style={{ fontSize: 16 }}>↻</span>

          {scheduledAt ? new Date(scheduledAt).getDate() : '—'}
        </strong>
        {' '}automaticamente.
      </span>
    </div>
  )}
</div>
```

Confirme que:
